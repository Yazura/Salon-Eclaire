Skin Repair

Advanced Treatments

Dermaplaning
45 Minutes
Dermaplaning is a simple and safe procedure for exfoliating the epidermis and ridding the skin of fine vellus hair (peach fuzz). There is no downtime with this treatment and the results are a more refined, smooth, and “glowing” appearance.

Ultra-Detox Chemical Peel
30-60 Minutes
The skin you want is under the skin you see. Reveal it with our powerful, non-surgical peel. You'll undo years of visible damage and premature age signs - without the redness, flaking, or actual "peeling" of the skin from a traditional peel. Our advanced peel technology removes the uppermost layers of the skin at a microscopic level, to immediately tighten, unclog pores, improve lines and leave skin with a younger, velvety-smooth texture. Plus, you'll benefit from detoxifying activated charcoal to draw out aging impurities. Best results achieved with a series of treatments - your esthetician will choose from three different acid levels to meet your skin's needs each time.
